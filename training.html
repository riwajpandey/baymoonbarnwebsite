Horse Training

Our Training Philosophy

At Bay Moon Barn, we believe in a holistic approach to horse training. Each horse is unique, and we tailor our training programs to meet individual needs, temperament, and goals. Our experienced trainers work with horses of all ages and experience levels, from young prospects to established competitors.

We focus on building a strong foundation, developing trust between horse and handler, and progressively advancing skills in a safe, supportive environment.

Our Training Process

1

Initial Consultation

We meet with you to understand your horse's background, current level, and your goals. This helps us create a customized training plan.

2

Assessment

Our trainers thoroughly evaluate your horse's temperament, physical condition, and current skills to establish a baseline.

3

Training Execution

We implement the training plan with regular updates and progress reports. Communication with owners is key to our success.

4

Progress Evaluation

We regularly assess progress and adjust the training plan as needed to ensure optimal results.

5

Conclusion & Transition

Upon completion, we provide detailed documentation and guidance for maintaining skills. Owner lessons available to ensure smooth transition.

Disciplines We Train

Dressage

Classical training emphasizing harmony, balance, and precision. We train from basic through advanced levels.

Jumping / Hunter

Jump training from beginner-friendly courses through advanced competition levels. Flat work and hunter classes included.

Western

Training in Western disciplines including trail, Western pleasure, reining, and ranch work.

Eventing

Three-phase training covering dressage, cross-country, and show jumping for event horses at all levels.

Trail & Conditioning

Trail training, endurance conditioning, and developing confident trail horses.

Problem Solving

Addressing behavioral issues, spooking, bucking, and other challenges to create safe, reliable partners.
