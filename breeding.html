Breeding Program

Developing quality horses through careful selection and management

Our Breeding Philosophy

At Bay Moon Barn, our breeding program is built on a foundation of careful selection, thoughtful planning, and a commitment to developing horses that combine athletic ability, sound temperament, and longevity. We breed with intention, not volume.

Every breeding decision is made with the future in mind — considering not only pedigree and conformation, but also temperament, health, and suitability for their intended purpose. We believe in breeding better horses for the future.

Our Breeding Goals

Temperament

We prioritize kind, willing, intelligent horses with good dispositions. A good temperament is the foundation of a successful partnership.

Athletic Ability

Developing horses with natural athletic ability, balance, and movement suitable for their intended discipline.

Soundness

Breeding for structural soundness and longevity. Sound conformation and good health are essential to a horse's career and quality of life.

Versatility

Creating horses that are adaptable and capable across multiple disciplines, providing owners with flexible partners.

Health

Comprehensive health screening and genetic considerations to promote long, healthy lives and reduce hereditary issues.

Partnership

Above all, we breed horses that genuinely enjoy working with people and form strong bonds with their owners.

Our Breeding Process

1

Selection

Careful evaluation and selection of breeding animals based on temperament, conformation, health, and genetic compatibility.

2

Health Screening

Comprehensive veterinary evaluations, genetic testing, and health screenings to ensure breeding animals are sound and healthy.

3

Planned Breeding

Strategic breeding decisions made with careful consideration of pedigrees, genetics, and desired outcomes.

4

Mare Care

Pregnant mares receive specialized nutrition, veterinary care, and management to ensure healthy foals.

5

Foal Development

Young foals receive excellent care, early handling, exposure, and training to develop into quality adult horses.
